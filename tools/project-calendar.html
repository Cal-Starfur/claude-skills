🎮 Gameplay Design Horizon — Wigglers Room
What fixing each P1/P2 actually unlocks for players
🔴 Fix PERF-1 + PERF-2 first
These are the frame-budget wall. Once trash chunks and pPath scans are pre-rendered/indexed, you have headroom for everything below. Players will feel the game get snappy — the bin comes alive.
🌊 Then fix ISS-13 (pooled)
The entire moisture economy is broken until this is verified. Once draining actually reduces pooled, the sweet-spot reward (0.3–0.5 = HP regen + gut efficiency) becomes a real loop players can discover and optimize.
🪱 FEAT-4 is the discoverability unlock
Long-press gesture scoping + drain/junction autopilot. Right now the junction and drain systems are nearly invisible to players. Autopilot makes them feel intentional and satisfying — a worm that goes and does the thing you pointed at.
🤝 FEAT-1 is the social moment
Cross-player tunnel clogging. Your poop blocking another worm's drain is the first time two players meaningfully interact. This is the Reddit-native mechanic — chaos, comedy, competition — all from the existing clog system.
📱 FEAT-2 removes returning-player friction
Device heartbeat + conflict UI. The #1 silent drop-off is players who left the game open on another tab coming back to a broken session. One heartbeat token + "Take over / Wait" UI fixes it entirely.
🌱 After PERF-1: richer bin is free
With trash render cost near zero you can safely double chunk count, add acid food speed boosts, rare golden scraps, and a "chunk discovery log" — 27 types × first-eat entries — without any frame penalty.
